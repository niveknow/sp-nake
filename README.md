### A Snake Game Starring **Sterling** 🧒✨

Hi! This game was made for a super cool 10-year-old guy named **Sterling** who loves video games and sushi!

---

## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Tap to start** — or press any arrow key
2. **Use arrow keys** (↑ ↓ ← →) or **W A S D** to steer the snake
3. **Eat the food** to grow!
4. **Don't hit walls** and **don't bite yourself** or it's game over!

---

## 🍣 The Food (YUM!)

Each time you eat, a **different treat** appears!

| Food | What it looks like | Sound |
|------|-------------------|-------|
| 🍣 **Sushi** | Salmon nigiri with rice | *ding-ding!* |
| 🍬 **Candy** | Bright wrapped candy | *sparkle!* |
| 🍨 **Ice Cream** | Pink scoop with cherry | *mmm-nom!* |

And the snake's head? That's **Sterling's face** — it rotates to look where he's going! 👀
